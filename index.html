The gap

Cotton is a ~$45B global market, yet hedging still depends on session-bound venues and fragmented regional basis. There is no dedicated 24/7 cotton perp built for how the industry actually operates.

What we build

A cotton perpetual DEX on Arbitrum: USDC collateral, ICE-referenced marks, and a pricing engine that uses order-book signals when external feeds go quiet — so the market does not sleep when ICE does.

Status

Soft launch in progress. Testnet preview is live for UX and pricing demos. Smart contracts and on-chain trading are not yet public. Details for investors and partners are in our white paper.

Built for commodity users

  3. Pricing — ICE Oracle when live, Order Book when it is not.
